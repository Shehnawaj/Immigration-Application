Business Visa

Business visa

A Business Visa is a non-immigrant visa that permits individuals to enter a foreign country temporarily for business-related activities. These activities may include attending business meetings, participating in conferences, negotiating contracts, or consulting with business associates.

The duration and specific permissions of a Business Visa vary by country, but they generally allow for short-term stays without the intent of permanent employment.

It's important to note that while a Business Visa facilitates various professional engagements, it does not authorize the holder to engage in regular employment within the host country.
